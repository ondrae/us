THE ADVENTURES OF ANDREW + ADRIENNE

Scroll ▾

San Francisco

They met at SF State and became better friends over the years. He was studying Urban Planning and she was in the Geography graduate program. The year was 2008. Fast forward four years, they start dating after dancing the night away together during Casey and Dimitri's wedding weekend.

Kansas City

He starts his year long fellowship with Code for America that takes him to Kansas City several times that year.

Grand Canyon

They take their first vacation together to the Grand Canyon via Las Vegas. In Vegas, they visit the Neon Museum and instantly fall in love with the place and its surrounding Downtown Las Vegas area.

Big Sur

She lands a new job with the SF Planning Department and they celebrate by staying in a vintage Airstream in Big Sur.

Mexico City

He completes his fellowship and leaves for San Miguel de Allende to join other fellows and they meet up in Mexico City.

Chicago

They share a fun weekend riding bikes, eating bibimbap, walking through small neighborhoods, stumbling upon music festivals, and getting rides home via pedicab.

Yelapa

Good friends Caroline and Scott wed in Yelapa and they basically have the entire beach to themselves with amazing sunsets and a friendly beach dog.

Los Angeles

They hang in Koreatown and greater Downtown Los Angeles and explore the area as well as visit with Daniella and company.

New York City

Fourth of July in NYC. They spend time with close friends and family and watch the fireworks from Brooklyn over the East River.

Minneapolis

She meets the parents and goes to her first Minnesota State Fair and successfully witnesses butter sculptures. He shows her around town and takes her to see his favorite local hip hop group.

Tokyo

She tags along on his work trip for a short 5-day trip. They’re taken around town by her good friend Noriko and enjoy ramen, laughs, lunchtime sized beers, and transit. Little did she know that he’d propose to her on their last morning on the 25th floor of their Shibuya hotel.

You

They'd be honored to have you join them from your home to their celebration in... wait for it...

Vegas

They’re tying the knot in DTLV! When they thought of places to wed, they couldn’t think of a better place than the beloved Neon Museum. They hope you enjoy this pocket of Vegas as much as they do.
