THE ADVENTURES OF ANDREW + ADRIENNE

Scroll ▾

San Francisco

Our young lovers met at SF State in 2008 becoming fast friends. He was studying Urban Planning and she was in the Geography graduate program. Many dance parties later, they knew they wanted to continue this dance.

Kansas City

He starts a year long fellowship with Code for America that takes him to Kansas City working in the Mayor's office. A crippling BBQ addiction and a new friendship with Ariel Kennan is formed.

Grand Canyon

They take their first vacation together to the Grand Canyon via Las Vegas. Grand hikes, crazy sunsets, and wandering elk. In Vegas, they visit the Neon Museum and instantly fall in love with the place and its surrounding Downtown Las Vegas area.

Chicago

They share a fun weekend riding bikes, eating bibimbap, walking through small neighborhoods, stumbling upon music festivals, and getting rides home via pedicab. Summer time Chi is something special.

Big Sur

She lands a new job with the SF Planning Department and they celebrate by staying in a vintage Airstream. They recite Jack Kerouac, dodge posion oak, and perfect cooking grilled garlic shrimp.

Mexico City

He completes his fellowship and leaves for some R&R with the other Fellows in San Miguel de Allende. She joins for Thanxgiving in Mexico City. Falling in love with the street food, and mezcal, they will never stop travelling.

Yelapa

Good friends Caroline and Scott wed in Yelapa and they basically have the entire beach to themselves with amazing sunsets and a friendly beach dog.

Los Angeles

They hang in Koreatown exploring, as well as visits with Daniella and company. Fancy hotels become a thing they do now.

New York City

Fourth of July in NYC. They spend time with close friends and family and watch the fireworks from Brooklyn over the East River.

Minneapolis

She meets the parents, goes to her first Minnesota State Fair, and becomes infatuated with the Princess Kay of the Milky Way Butter Sculptures. He shows her around town and takes her to see some local rap.

Tokyo

She tags along on his short work trip. They’re taken around town by her good friend Noriko and enjoy ramen, laughs, lunchtime sized beers, and transit. Little did she know that he'd propose to her on their last morning on the 25th floor of their Shibuya hotel. Over smoked salmon and pickled plums, a promise was made.

You

Andrew and Adrienne would be honored to have you join them from your home to their celebration in ... wait for it ...

Downtown Las Vegas

They’re tying the knot in DTLV! When they thought of places to wed, they couldn’t think of a better place than the beloved Neon Museum. This ain't the Strip. It's an experiment in place making. They hope you will enjoy this pocket of Vegas as much as they do.
