WEEKEND THEME/LOOK AND FEEL

Ha! It is probably no surprise to most of you that we have a theme. Our goal is to have you channel your "inner neon" in whatever capacity suits you best. FRIDAY, MAY 8TH

12PM - POOL TIME

Downtown Grand Hotel Pool “Picnic,” 206 N 3rd St

Come say hello and hang poolside with us! We plan on chilling in a cabana all day starting at noon. Enjoy sun, pool, good company, and refreshments!


SATURDAY, MAY 9TH

5PM - SHUTTLE PICKUP

Downtown Grand Hotel Lobby, 206 N 3rd St

There will be a shuttle to transport guests to the ceremony and back. The shuttle will take two trips from the hotel and will ensure guests arrive at least 15 minutes before the start of the ceremony.


6PM - CEREMONY

Neon Museum, 770 Las Vegas Blvd North


Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the ceremony to check in at the La Concha Visitor Center upon arrival. The ceremony will begin promptly at 6pm and will be standing room only with a few benches for those who must be seated. If you decide to drive, parking is available in the lot off of McWilliams Avenue.

After the ceremony you have the option to take the first shuttle trip (around 6:30PM) or to hang around while we take couple/family photos and board the second shuttle trip back to the hotel (around 7PM). The reception is a short 6-minute walk from the hotel.


7:30PM - RECEPTION

Le Thai, 523 E. Fremont Street


Le Thai is a fun and casual restaurant that we will have all to ourselves for a few hours. This will be a buffet dinner with open seating. Join us for food, drinks, thoughtful words, cake, and fun!


9:30PM - AFTER PARTY

Commonwealth, 525 E. Fremont Street

The Commonwealth is conveniently located directly next door to Le Thai. This is the adults only part of the celebration. Please mention you are with "Adrienne and Andrew's Wedding Party" and you'll get a wristband and a waived entrance fee. Head to the rooftop where we have several tables reserved just for us.


SUNDAY, MAY 10TH

1PM - MORE POOL TIME

Downtown Grand Hotel Pool “Picnic,” 206 N 3rd St

After you’ve rolled out of bed and maybe had your breakfast or brunch, make your way back to the pool to unwind and share stories of the night before. FYI - they serve food and drink at the pool too. We will be around all Sunday and leave for our honeymoon early Monday morning. So, please stop by to say hello and/or goodbye.
